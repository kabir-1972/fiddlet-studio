Built by players,

Fiddlet Studio is a scrappy, independent game studio on a mission to prove that the best games don't need massive budgets — they need genuine passion, sharp design, and a stubborn commitment to fun.

We make the kind of games that get stuck in your head at 2am. If you've ever lost an hour to a "quick puzzle," you know exactly what we're about.

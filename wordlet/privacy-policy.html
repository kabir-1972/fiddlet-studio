This policy explains how Wordlet collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We believe in plain language — so we've written this to be actually readable. If something is unclear, reach out and we'll explain it.

01 — Overview
Who we
are

Wordlet is a word puzzle application designed to make vocabulary fun, engaging, and mentally stimulating. This Privacy Policy applies to the Wordlet mobile application and any associated services we provide.

We are the data controller responsible for your personal information. By using Wordlet, you agree to the practices described in this document.

The short version: We collect only what we need to run the app, we don't sell your data, and we give you real control over your information.

02 — Collection
What we
collect

We collect information in two ways — what you provide directly, and what we observe when you use the app.

Data typeExamplesHow collected
Account info Name, email address You provide it at signup
Game progress Scores, streaks, completed puzzles Recorded as you play
Device info OS version, device model, locale Collected automatically
Usage data Features used, session duration Collected automatically
Crash logs Error traces, app state at crash Collected automatically

We do not collect payment information directly — all purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We never access your device camera, microphone, or contacts.

03 — Usage
How we
use it

Every piece of data we collect has a specific, defined purpose. We don't collect data speculatively or "just in case."

  • Operate and maintain your account and sync your progress across devices
  • Personalize your experience — difficulty calibration, word selection, streaks
  • Diagnose bugs and improve app stability using crash and error reports
  • Send you optional notifications (only if you've enabled them)
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service
  • Conduct aggregate, anonymized analytics to understand how features perform

We never use your data to build advertising profiles or sell insights to third parties.

04 — Sharing
Who sees
your data

We don't sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Period. We share data only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers who help us operate Wordlet — cloud hosting, crash analytics, and customer support tools — under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Business transfers — in the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, your data would transfer with appropriate notice and the same privacy protections

Leaderboards: If you choose to participate in public leaderboards, your username and score are visible to other players. You can opt out in Settings → Privacy at any time.

05 — Storage
How we
protect it

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We apply role-based access controls, so only team members who need your data to do their job can access it.

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely — but it cannot be traced back to you.

06 — Your rights
You're in
control

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data

Most actions can be taken directly in the app under Settings → Privacy → My Data. For other requests, contact us and we'll respond within 30 days.

07 — Children
Under
13 policy

Wordlet is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take action within 48 hours.

08 — Changes
Policy
updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we'll notify you via in-app notification or email at least 14 days before the changes take effect.

Continued use of Wordlet after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We'll always keep a changelog of previous versions accessible on request.

Questions about privacy?

Our team is happy to help — we respond within 2 business days.
